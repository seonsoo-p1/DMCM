DMCM: Disentangled Multi-Context Meta-Learning

Seonsoo Kim*, Jungill Kang*, Taehong Kim, Seongil Hong
Agency for Defense Development (ADD)
CoRL 2025
*Equal contribution
Paper Supplementary Code arXiv

Robust zero-shot recomposition across disentangled contexts.

Abstract

We propose DMCM, a meta-learning framework that explicitly disentangles multiple task context factors, such as robot properties and terrain variations. By isolating these factors into separate latent vectors, our method achieves improved robustness, interpretability, and zero-shot transfer in both synthetic regression and quadruped locomotion tasks.
